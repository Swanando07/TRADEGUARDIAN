Questions

Before you ask

Does this connect to my broker or prop firm?

No. Trade Guardian never connects to any broker or account. You update your balance yourself after each trade — nothing is read automatically, nothing is sent anywhere.

What happens to my data?

Everything is stored locally in your browser. Uninstalling the extension deletes it permanently.

Can I change my rules later?

Yes — target, drawdown, trailing balance, loss limit, and lock durations are all editable anytime in Settings.

Does it work on Brave?

Yes, and any other Chromium-based browser.
